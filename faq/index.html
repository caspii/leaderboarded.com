Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about keepTheScore

Getting Started

How do I create a leaderboard? +

Visit keepTheScore.com and click "Create Leaderboard". Choose your type, add players, customize the design, and share the link. Takes less than 2 minutes!

Is it really free? +

Yes! Create unlimited leaderboards with up to 50 players each, real-time updates, and basic customization - all free. Premium plans add more players and advanced features.

Do I need an account? +

No account required to start! Create and share leaderboards instantly. Sign up only if you want to save and manage multiple boards.

Features

Can multiple people update scores? +

Yes! Share admin links with team members, enable player self-scoring, or allow public submissions. All updates appear instantly.

Can I customize the design? +

Absolutely! Add your logo, choose colors, upload player images, select themes, and more. Make it match your brand perfectly.
